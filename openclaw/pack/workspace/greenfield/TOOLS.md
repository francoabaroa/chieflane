## Chieflane Surface Tools

### surface_publish
Create or replace a persistent shell surface for work worth reopening later.

### surface_patch
Update an existing shell surface by `surfaceKey`.

### surface_close
Archive or close a shell surface once complete.

## Chieflane Usage Rules

- Prefer persistent surfaces for drafts, queues, dossiers, meeting prep, and digests.
- Every surface must include a concise fallback summary.
- Reuse `surfaceKey` where possible.
